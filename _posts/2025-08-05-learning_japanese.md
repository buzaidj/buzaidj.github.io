I’m learning Japanese. I started last November. Earlier this year I promised myself I’d stop when I could read comfortably read NHK Web News Easy (やさしい). I got there and didn’t stop. That was a while ago. Since then I've read a few novels, finished the Quartet textbook series, and am now studying for the N2.

I've devoted a lot of my free time into this I'm really not far in the journey, but I'm surprised I even got as far as I am, and I might as well keep going. Compared to my life before I picked up this habit, I feel I have less time idly think, something I once had too much time to do. I like not feeling idle.
